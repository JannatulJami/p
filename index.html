Japanese Strawberry Cream Cake
By Jannatul Jami

If your me you know I LOVE strawberries. they're the best fruit to me. And we all love cake so lets combine the two! Japanese Starebrry cream cake will be the sweetest thing eversince strawberries existed.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Washed and Dried Strawberries
  • Cake Flour
  • 3 Large eggs
  • Honey
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt
  • Vegtable Oil
  • 2 seprate bowls of sugar
  • Baking Powder

Instructions

  1. Mix the egg yolks, honey, sugar, and salt until it becomes fluffy and yellow color
  2. Mix the egg whites in a seprate bowl so that to become a meringue and that the tips fall but still in shape.
  3. Add half the egg whites with the first mixture and gently fold it with each other.
  4. Combine cake flour and baking powder and sift it in the egg yolk and egg whit mixture.
  5. Add oil and and remain half egg whites to the mixture and gently fold it.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees Farenheit in parchemnt lined cake pans.
  7. Slice even layers and brush sugur syrup
  8. Add whipe cream and then strawberries on top and cover in with more whipped cream and then add another cake layer and rpeat until you run out of cake layers.
  9. Frost the top of the cake then decorate with more strawberries.
